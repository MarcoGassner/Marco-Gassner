About

I am a research assistant and doctoral candidate at the University Hospital of the Technical University of Munich. My work focuses on researching and implementing digital mental health interventions.

I hold two master’s degrees: one in Philosophy (LMU Munich) and one in an interdisciplinary program in Psychology and Educational Science (TUM).
